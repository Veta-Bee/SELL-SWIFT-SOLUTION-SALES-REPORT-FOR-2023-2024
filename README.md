**Sales Amount by Payment Method**


Credit Card transactions lead in sales amount ($1,757,563.52), followed closely by Bank Transfers ($1,717,363.17), while Cash transactions are the lowest ($1,544,338.54). **Customer Type by Sales Amount**


Sales from returning customers ($2,513,006.93) and new customers ($2,506,258.30) are nearly equal, indicating a well-balanced customer base. This suggests strong customer retention while also attracting new buyers. To sustain growth, strategies should focus on maintaining loyalty while expanding outreach to new markets.

**Sales Channel by Sales Amount**


Retail sales ($2,560,431.30) slightly outperform online sales ($2,458,833.93), indicating a strong physical store presence. However, the close margin suggests that online sales are nearly as significant, highlighting the importance of digital channels. A balanced approach to both retail and online strategies can maximize overall sales performance.

**Sales Amount by Date**


Sales remained relatively stable throughout 2023, with a peak in Q4 ($1,320,505.26), indicating strong year-end performance.
